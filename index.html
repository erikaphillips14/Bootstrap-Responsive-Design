My Top 5 Dog Breeds

On this Website, I will be sharing my top five dog breeds of all time!

Start scrolling!

#1 The Poochon

The Poochon is my favorite breed because they are so adorable and my dog is a Poochon! The Poochon is a mix of Bichon and Poodle and are considered a designer dog. They are a small to medium sized breed and are great companions.

Characteristics:

  • Hypoallergenic
  • Friendly
  • Playful
  • Good with kids

#2 The Corgi

Corgis, with their stubby legs and big personalities make them my second favorite dog breed. They are extremely friendly and playful dogs. One of my favorite characteristics about Corgis are their ears!

Characteristics:

  • Friendly
  • Playful
  • Loyal
  • Intelligent

#3 The Labradoodle

The Labradoodle is my third favorite dog because I also used to own a Labradoodle. They are full of energy and are extremely sweet and friendly. This breed is a mix between the Labrador Retriever and a Poodle and you will love all of the affection that they give!.

Characteristics:

  • Athletic
  • Affectionate
  • Social
  • Playful

#4 The Pomski

This unique blend between the Siberian Husky and a Pomeranian makes for a stunning breed known as the Pomski. They are a small to medium sized dog breed and their beauty catches everyone's eyes. They make as great companions and are extremely social dogs. .

Characteristics:

  • Good Health
  • Easy to Train
  • Social
  • Energetic

#5 The Samoyed

My number five dog breed is the Samoyed! They are beautiful dogs with their white fluffy coat. Samoyed's are friendly and playful dogs and a great family dog. The Samoyed may look big, but in reality they are big fluffy sweethearts .

Characteristics:

  • Good with Kids
  • Affectionate
  • Adventurous
  • Fluffy
